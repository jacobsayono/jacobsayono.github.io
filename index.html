Why This Project?

˙ ˙ ˙

   I want to create an autonomous product for my university before I graduate. This has been a long-term goal in my heart for a while. This is a non-profit project. In this venture, I developed 4 teams: Mechanical, Electrical, Software, and Autonomy.

   Each team is given its own quarterly goals and iteration phases to progress weekly together so that no team lags behind. I took the challenge to lead the Autonomy team as I was unable to find another undergraduate at my university with intensive robotics autonomy experience.

   I inspire everyone at BruinBot to not give up as I oversee all teams as Product Manager to tie a multi-disciplinary project together. I could not have done this without my amazing team and the help from open-source projects contributed by our cherished robotics community.

   From your mobile app to your robot, this end-to-end product is made for UCLA, by UCLA. This project is funded out-of-pocket from personal scholarship grants and awards. I am taking a risk here because there is nothing else I'd rather do. Thank you to those who support this project.
