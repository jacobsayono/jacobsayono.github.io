Why This Project?

˙ ˙ ˙

   I want to create an autonomous product for my university before I graduate. This has been a long-term goal in my heart for a while. This is a non-profit project. In this venture, I developed 4 teams: Mechanical, Electrical, Software, and Autonomy.

   Each team is given their own quarterly goals and iteration phases to progress weekly together so that no team lacks behind. I took the challenge to lead the Autonomy team as I was unable to find another undergraduate at my university with intensive robotics autonomy experience.
